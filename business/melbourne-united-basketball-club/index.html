Melbourne United Basketball Club

Melbourne United is a professional men’s basketball team in the Australian National Basketball League (NBL). Our purpose is to deliver opportunities for sustained success both on and off the court whilst providing the highest level of engagement and entertainment in all we do.

Formed in 2014 after playing in the NBL as the Melbourne Tigers for 30 years, Melbourne United is the premier NBL team in Victoria. During each NBL season, Melbourne United play up to 14 home games at John Cain Arena. The Club plays in front of up to 10,500 fans at home games.

In 2018 the Club won its first Championship as United, and in 2021 they won their second.
