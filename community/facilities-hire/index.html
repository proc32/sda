Facilities Hire at Salesian College Chadstone

Our Spaces

Are you interested in booking space for your next event, function or sporting game? With a range of venues for hire, Salesian College Chadstone has the following facilities available.

  • Kimberley Hall – suitable for events, volleyball, badminton (please note, this is an undersized basketball court)
  • Outdoor Covered courts – outdoor covered courts, suitable for basketball, futsal and netball
  • Bosco Oval (including change rooms) – suitable for football and cricket with two available cricket nets
  • Mannix Oval – suitable for soccer

If you are interesting in hiring a College venue, or would like to receive more information, please complete the form below and we’ll get back to you.
